MY TOP THREE MOVIE PICKS

By Javan Fraser

1. TOP GUN: MAVERICK

This movie is a classic American film that features all
my favourite genres. Fighter jet action? Love it! Drama and
alittle bit of comedy? Amazingggg! The soundtrack? Immaculate!


2. FAST AND FURIOUS 5

Within the FF sequel, this movie in my opinion, is the best one
yet. Everything seems realistic enough. The action, the drama,
the plot are all amazingly put together. As we all know, after this one,
every other one that came was a complete bust, the laws of physics were
absolutely violated and reality just went right out the door.


3. THE HITMAN'S BODYGUARD

Now this movie has got to be one of the funniest movies I have ever
watched in my life. It features two of my favorite actors,
Ryan Reynolds and Samuel Jackson. The plot is centered around Reynolds
acting as a bodyguard to the protect the life of Infamous hitman, Darius Kincaid(Jackson).
